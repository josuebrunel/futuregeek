Hi guys !! I'm Josue Kouka (yeah i know, tough to pronounce), a young congolese free software and open-source enthusiast, fond of programming and thirsty of knowledge.

This little blog is about stuff that i like, by that i mean a lot of Linux and Programming (yeah sounds like fun ^_^).
